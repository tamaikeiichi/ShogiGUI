Privacy Policy for ShogiGUI
Last Updated: May 13, 2026

ShogiGUI (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is an Android application developed by Keiichi Tamai (hereinafter referred to as "the Developer"). The Developer is committed to protecting the privacy of users (hereinafter referred to as "Users") and ensuring a secure experience.

1. Information Collection and Use
The App is designed to prioritize user privacy. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location data.

Game Data (Kifu): Any game records or Shogi positions imported from the clipboard or created within the App are stored locally on the User's device. This data is never uploaded to any external servers.

Engine Logs: Debug logs generated by the Shogi engines (e.g., USI engine outputs) are saved only in the App’s private directory on the device and are not accessible by the Developer.

2. Permissions
The App may request certain permissions to function correctly:

Storage Access: Used solely to read engine files (evaluation functions/networks) and write debug logs for the User’s own review.

Clipboard Access: Used only when the User explicitly triggers the "Import" function to load game data (KIF/CSA/USI formats).

3. Third-Party Services
The App does not use third-party advertising networks, analytics tools, or SDKs that track user behavior. Therefore, no data is shared with third parties for marketing or tracking purposes.

4. Security
While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, the App follows industry-standard practices by keeping all processing and data storage local to the device, minimizing the risk of data breaches.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
The Developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page or within the App.

6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact the Developer via the contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing.
